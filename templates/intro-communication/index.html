What is Magnet Fishing?

Magnet Fishing is an exciting, growing sport, which involves 'casting' magnets into canals, lakes, off bridges and piers, in the hope of connecting to metal objects. 'Catches' can range from old nails, beer bottle tops, bikes, scooters or even treasure!

Magnet Fishing is an easy sport to take up. All you need is a strong magnet and rope and you’re ready to go! Such items can be purchased at many outlets online, often providing international shipping options.

This Website serves to bring fellow Magnet fishers together and enables this community to share their finds throughout the Stockholm area. You can input your ‘Catches’ at Add Catches and you can share them with others at Catch Log.

So start your adventure in Magnet Fishing today.

Connect, share, and most of all, have fun!
